MSEs encounters challenges from increased competitions to technological change, and some of them have difficulties in adapting to these changes making them more vulnerable to some problems. All of these transactions result in customer and employee’s time, effort and even revenue being wasted when it can be done using an automated cashiering system.

Developing a Cashiering System for these SMEs may somehow solve these problems in making it easy for both parties. An automated cashiering system for Micro and Small Enterprise is a Software as a Service (SaaS) platform wherein the user accepts and calculates payments from the customer, and print their bills or receipts. Moreover, the business owner tracks and manage the flow of inventory and review inventory and sales reports for business decision purposes.
